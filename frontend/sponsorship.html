Advertising & Sponsorship Policy

Last updated: 17 March 2026

1. Our Commitment to Clinical Integrity

ClinIQ is a clinical decision-support tool used by qualified homeopathic practitioners. We recognise that the trust our users place in us depends on the integrity and objectivity of our platform. This policy outlines our approach to advertising, sponsorship, and commercial partnerships.

2. No Third-Party Advertising

ClinIQ does not display third-party advertisements within the application interface. Our AI-generated recommendations, remedy rankings, and clinical outputs are never influenced by advertising, sponsorship, or commercial partnerships.

We do not:

  • Serve display ads, banner ads, or pop-up advertisements
  • Insert sponsored content into clinical results or recommendations
  • Allow paid placement or prioritisation of specific remedies or products
  • Sell or share user data with advertisers

3. Sponsorship Guidelines

ClinIQ may, in the future, enter into sponsorship or partnership arrangements with organisations in the homeopathic and healthcare ecosystem. Any such partnerships will be governed by these principles:

  • Transparency: All sponsored content, if any, will be clearly and prominently labelled as such
  • Independence: Sponsorships will never influence AI outputs, remedy recommendations, or clinical content
  • Relevance: Partners must be relevant to the homeopathic or healthcare community
  • Ethical Standards: We will not partner with organisations that promote unverified health claims, misleading treatments, or practices that conflict with classical homeopathic methodology

4. Educational Content

ClinIQ may publish or feature educational content, articles, or webinars in collaboration with homeopathic institutions, practitioners, or researchers. Such content will always be:

  • Clearly attributed to the author or institution
  • Editorially independent of any commercial arrangement
  • Reviewed for clinical accuracy before publication

5. Affiliate & Referral Programmes

ClinIQ may offer referral programmes where existing users can recommend the platform to colleagues. Any referral incentives will be disclosed transparently and will not affect the referring practitioner's access to features or the quality of recommendations provided.

6. Pharmaceutical & Remedy Manufacturer Relationships

ClinIQ does not have commercial relationships with homeopathic pharmaceutical manufacturers or remedy suppliers. Remedy recommendations are generated solely from classical homeopathic reference texts and AI pattern analysis, without any commercial bias.

Should partnerships with remedy suppliers be established in the future, they will:

  • Be limited to supply-chain convenience (e.g., ordering links) and clearly separated from clinical recommendations
  • Never influence remedy rankings or AI-generated outputs
  • Be disclosed transparently to all users

7. Revenue Model

ClinIQ is funded through subscription plans paid directly by practitioners. Our revenue model is intentionally designed to align our incentives with user value rather than advertising revenue. This ensures that our product decisions are driven by clinical utility, not ad impressions.

8. Reporting Concerns

If you believe any content on ClinIQ is biased, undisclosed sponsored content, or otherwise conflicts with this policy, please report it to:

support@homeocliniq.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our business evolves. Material changes will be communicated to users. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

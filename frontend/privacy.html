Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 March 2026

1. Introduction

ClinIQ Private Limited ("ClinIQ", "we", "us", or "our") operates the ClinIQ platform, an AI-powered clinical decision-support tool for qualified homeopathic practitioners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

By using ClinIQ, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you register, we collect your full name, email address, and a hashed version of your password. We never store passwords in plain text.

2.2 Query & Clinical Data

When you submit a symptom query, we process the text you provide to generate remedy recommendations. Query history, saved rubrics, and patient records are stored in your account and are accessible only to you.

2.3 Voice Data

If you use the voice input feature, audio is temporarily processed for transcription and is not permanently stored on our servers. Transcribed text is treated as query data.

2.4 Payment Information

Payments are processed by Razorpay. We do not store your card numbers, UPI IDs, or banking credentials. We retain only transaction IDs, plan details, and payment status for billing records.

2.5 Usage Data

We may collect non-personal technical data such as browser type, device type, IP address, and access timestamps to improve service performance and security.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the ClinIQ service
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To process your queries and generate AI-powered recommendations
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send essential service notifications (account security, billing)
  • To improve our algorithms, platform stability, and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or clinical queries with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on servers located in India (AWS Mumbai, ap-south-1 region). We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • JWT-based authentication with short-lived access tokens
  • Per-user data isolation — no practitioner can access another's data
  • Bcrypt password hashing
  • Regular security reviews and updates

5. Data Retention

Your account data, query history, saved rubrics, and patient records are retained for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will permanently remove all associated data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenRouter / Google Gemini — for AI-powered query processing. Query text is sent to the LLM provider for response generation. No personally identifiable information is sent alongside queries.
  • Razorpay — for payment processing. Subject to Razorpay's Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — for infrastructure hosting.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored with us
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your query history and patient records
  • Withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@homeocliniq.com.

8. Cookies

ClinIQ uses only essential, functional storage (localStorage) to persist your authentication tokens and theme preference. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

ClinIQ is a professional tool intended for use by qualified medical practitioners only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or an in-app notification. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
